
# EXECORA — Subscription Plans & Feature Division
## Startup-Ready Pricing & Packaging Strategy

---

## Overview

EXECORA is offered through **tiered annual subscription plans**, designed to:
- Support pilot adoption
- Encourage natural upsell
- Protect core differentiators
- Scale from single projects to enterprise portfolios

Pricing is **per project / per year**, ensuring clarity, predictability, and executive-friendly budgeting.

---

## Plan 1 — EXECORA CORE

**Positioning:**  
Execution visibility and operational control foundation.

**Target Customers:**
- Pilot projects
- Small to mid-sized projects
- First-time digital adoption

**Annual Subscription:**  
USD 15,000 – 20,000 / project

### Included Modules
- Project & Organization Management
- Smart Daily Operations
- Activities (Execution Layer)
- Issues & Risk Management (basic)
- In-app Notifications
- Basic Audit Trail
- Standard Dashboards

### Excluded
- Workflow Engine
- Inspections & Quality Management
- NCR & Quality Intelligence
- BIM & 3D Viewer Integration
- Advanced Analytics
- Email Notifications

**Value Statement:**  
Replace spreadsheets and disconnected tools with a single execution hub.

---

## Plan 2 — EXECORA PROFESSIONAL (Flagship)

**Positioning:**  
Controlled execution with enforced workflows.

**Target Customers:**
- Medium to large projects
- Digitally mature contractors
- Projects with quality and risk exposure

**Annual Subscription:**  
USD 30,000 – 40,000 / project

### Included Modules
Everything in CORE, plus:
- Workflow Engine
- Inspections & Quality Management
- NCR & Quality Intelligence
- SLA Enforcement & Escalations
- BIM & 3D Viewer Integration (APS)
- Advanced Dashboards & Insights
- Email Notifications
- Full Audit Trail

**Value Statement:**  
Execution is not just tracked — it is enforced.

---

## Plan 3 — EXECORA ENTERPRISE

**Positioning:**  
Portfolio-level execution governance.

**Target Customers:**
- Tier-1 contractors
- Multi-project programs
- Corporate PMO and governance teams

**Annual Subscription:**
- USD 60,000 – 80,000 / project  
or  
- USD 250,000+ / year (portfolio license)

### Included Modules
Everything in PROFESSIONAL, plus:
- Portfolio Dashboards
- Cross-Project Analytics
- Custom Workflow Design
- Advanced Compliance & Audit Reporting
- Dedicated Support & Onboarding
- Priority Roadmap Influence

**Value Statement:**  
Enterprise execution control at scale.
